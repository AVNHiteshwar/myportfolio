Classification of Solar Panel Defects

This project focuses on identifying and classifying defects in solar panels using machine learning techniques to enhance energy efficiency and reduce maintenance costs.

Key Highlights:

  • Collected and preprocessed image datasets of defective and non-defective solar panels.
  • Applied Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for defect detection and classification.
  • Achieved high classification accuracy through hyperparameter tuning.

Tools & Technologies: Python, TensorFlow, Keras, OpenCV, Matplotlib.
